ANNIE CHAEHONG LEE


I'm a junior at Washington University in St. Louis majoring in computer science and applied mathematics and minoring in communication design. I'm interested in studying augmented reality and virtual reality for advanced communication experiences for people. I plan to attend a graduate school and study fields of computer science more in depth to improve my knowledge on machine learning, user experience, computer vision, and much more.

Something that differentiates me from other computer scientists is that I have a deep personal history with art and design. I used to pursue a degree as an artist, following my mother's and grandmother's career, but I switched my career path after realizing I can be just as creative with just a few lines of code--without paint and brushes. But still, this background in art and design lets me percieve the world more fluently, allowing me to build a better code that can communciate with others better.
